How do I sign in to my account?

Sign in to your account using your credentials.

How do I fund my wallet?

Navigate to the "Transfer" section, find wallet addresses for BTC, USDT TRC20, and ERC20. Choose one based on your preference and fund your wallet. Enter the amount, provide the wallet address if needed, and wait for confirmation. Your account balance will be updated after confirmation.

How do I explore available assets?

Head to the dashboard and locate the "Assets" section. Click on any property to view details.

How do I invest in a property?

On the property details page, scroll down to find the investment form. Enter the amount and click "Invest."

How do I view my investments?

After investing, check your investment details on the investment page. View assets, interest, percentages, etc.
